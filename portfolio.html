MY WORK
SKILLS:
With 10 years of experience in Photoshop, my skills have been honed from a young age, starting when I was just a child. Over the years, I've mastered a wide range of techniques, from basic image editing to advanced compositing, retouching, and graphic design. My deep familiarity with Photoshop's tools and features allows me to work efficiently and creatively, producing high-quality visual content that meets professional standards.
With 3 years of experience in Adobe Illustrator, I've developed a solid foundation in vector graphic design. Over this time, I've become proficient in creating detailed illustrations, logos, and typography. My understanding of Illustrator's tools and functions allows me to efficiently craft scalable, high-quality designs that maintain their clarity across various formats. This experience has equipped me to tackle a wide range of design projects with creativity and precision.
With a year of experience in Adobe XD, I've gained a strong grasp of designing user interfaces and crafting seamless user experiences. During this time, I've learned to efficiently create wireframes, prototypes, and interactive designs that are both visually appealing and functional. My familiarity with XD's collaborative features and design tools enables me to produce intuitive and responsive designs tailored to user needs.
With a year of experience in Adobe Animate, I've focused on creating game UI and item sprites. During this time, I've honed my skills in designing and animating game interfaces and assets, ensuring they are both visually appealing and functional. My experience with keyframe animation and timeline management allows me to produce polished and responsive designs that enhance the overall gaming experience.
I gained a foundational understanding of Python during my university studies, where it was part of my coursework. However, my skills were significantly advanced through a specialized course led by Developers Lab MNE. This experience allowed me to dive deeper into Python's capabilities, focusing on practical applications and real-world problem-solving. Through this course, I developed strong proficiency in writing clean, efficient code, and gained hands-on experience with various Python libraries and frameworks.
I studied HTML during my university coursework, where I developed more than a basic understanding of the language. My skills enable me to create and structure well-organized web content, with a good grasp of semantic elements and best practices. While my experience primarily stems from my studies, I am comfortable working with HTML and can build functional and responsive web pages.
I was introduced to CSS during my university studies, where it was part of the curriculum. While I have a basic understanding of CSS and can apply it effectively for styling web pages, my knowledge is at an okay level, primarily rooted in what I learned during the course.
I have a basic level of knowledge in JavaScript, acquired through my university coursework. This foundational understanding allows me to handle simple scripting tasks and implement basic interactivity on web pages. While my experience is limited, I am familiar with essential JavaScript concepts and can apply them to straightforward projects.
